Why Soccer Matters to Me

Soccer has been one of the biggest influences in my life. Growing up in Hawaiʻi, becoming captain of my high school team, serving a mission in Brazil, and now pursuing my goal of playing collegiate soccer at BYU have taught me discipline, resilience, leadership, and faith.
